  About me

ANA PETROV DJ

Ana Petrov is one the few female DJs that radiates with her presence on the scene. Traveling all around the world and visiting all the best clubs and music festivals, she became a hardcore clubber. She fell in love with electronic music, and then she decided to become a DJ herself and chase the world scene dream. In her DJ mixes, she plays Melodic House & Techno, Indian Dance, Tech House, Deep House, House... She does both Day & Night parties.
